Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa, known for its rich culture, diverse landscapes, and vibrant cities. It is the most populous country in Africa and has a growing economy driven by industries such as oil, agriculture, and technology. Nigeria is also famous for its music, film industry (Nollywood), and delicious cuisine. The country is home to both bustling urban centers and serene rural areas, with cities like Lagos and Abuja being major economic hubs, along with natural wonders such as the Niger River and the Zuma Rock.
